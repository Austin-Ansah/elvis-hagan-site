About

Prof. Elvis John Hagan Jr. is a development scholar and policy adviser at Bielefeld University, Germany, specializing in education systems, youth development, and sport for social change. With extensive research experience across sub-Saharan Africa and Europe, he advises government ministries, international organizations, and NGOs on evidence-based policy design and implementation. His work bridges academic rigor with practical solutions, addressing critical challenges in educational equity, skills development, and sport as a tool for peace and inclusion. Prof. Hagan's research has informed policy frameworks in multiple African nations and European development programs. He regularly collaborates with UNESCO, AU bodies, and national education ministries, translating complex research into actionable strategies that drive sustainable development outcomes.
